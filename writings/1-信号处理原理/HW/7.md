求 $x(n)=\{1,2,3,4\}$ 的 4 点 DFT 和 8 点 DFT

**4 点 DFT**
$$
X(k)=\sum_{n=0}^{3}{x(n)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{4}kn}} \\ 
\begin{align}
X(0) &= \sum_{n=0}^{3}{x(n)e^{-j \cdot \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot 0n}} = \sum{x(n)}=10 \\
X(1) &= \sum_{n=0}^{3}{x(n)e^{-j \cdot \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot n}} = -2+2j \\
X(2) &= \sum_{n=0}^{3}{x(n)e^{-j \cdot \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot 2n}} = -2 \\
X(3) &= \sum_{n=0}^{3}{x(n)e^{-j \cdot \frac{\pi}{2} \cdot 3n}} = -2-2j \\
\end{align}
$$
**8 点 DFT**

对原信号延拓得 $x(n)=\{1,2,3,4,0,0,0,0\}$
$$
X(k) = \sum_{n=0}^{7}{x(n)e^{-j\frac{\pi}{4}kn}} \\
\begin{array}{ll}
&X(0) = 10 \quad &X(1) = (1-\sqrt2) + (-3-3\sqrt2)j \\
&X(2) = -2+2j \quad &X(3) = (1+\sqrt2) + (+3-3\sqrt2)j \\
&X(4) = -2 \quad &X(5) = (1+\sqrt2) + (-3+3\sqrt2)j \\
&X(6) = -2-2j \quad &X(7) = (1-\sqrt2) + (+3+3\sqrt2)j \\
\end{array}
$$
