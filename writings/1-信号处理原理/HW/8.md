由 DFT 的计算公式, 有
$$
\begin{align}
X(k) 
&=  \sum_{n=0}^{N-1}{x(n)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}nk}} \\ 
&=  \sum_{m=0}^{\frac{N}{2}-1}{\left[ 
    x(2m)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k \cdot 2m} +
    x(2m+1)e^ {-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k \cdot (2m+1)} 
\right]} \\
&=  \sum_{m=0}^{\frac{N}2-1}{g(m)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k \cdot 2m}} +
    \sum_{m=0}^{\frac{N}2-1}{h(m)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k \cdot 2m}e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k}} \\
&=  \sum_{m=0}^{\frac{N}2-1}{g(m)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N/2}km}} + 
    e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N}k} \cdot \sum_{m=0}^{\frac{N}2-1}{h(m)e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{N/2}km}} \\
&=  G(k) + W_N^k \cdot H(k) \quad ,k=0,1,\dots,\frac{N}{2}-1
\end{align}
$$
